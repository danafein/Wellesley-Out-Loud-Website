Our Mission

    Wellesley Out Loud has two main (actionable) goals:

  1. to create a poetry-centered community at Wellesley College through open mics, events, and writing groups through collaboration and

  2. to bring more poets and performers to Wellesley campus.

Wellesley Out Loud wants to be the beginning of conversations on campus about things like being a POC, mental health and queer identities by giving students a place to share their narratives and frustrations. We want to give students an outlet and a space to write about their thoughts and feelings by creating a community and to inspire students with the work of their peers as well as outside performers.
